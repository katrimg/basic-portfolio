Marketing and Graphic Design Projects


Here are some examples of work completed at Arizona State University, during my marketing internship with Bridal by Blake Patricia, and independantly.


Club Hunter Website and App Design



Website Mockup

The mockup of the Club Hunter website design can be viewed to the left. The website is designed to allow users to view reviews for bars and clubs based on desired demographics & interests of reviewers. This allows users to see reviews from only individuals in their age group with similar interests to them.
I've also included some of the brainstorms for the mockup above the 4 pages. These were options for the home page, and alternatives for the login.




The App version of Club Hunter. After creating the mockup for the website, it seemed like an app would be a better platform. I redesigned my original layout increasing usability, and simplifying the search process. Both designs were created using Illustrator.
